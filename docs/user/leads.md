# Lead Management Module

## Overview
The Leads module manages potential customers through the sales pipeline from initial contact to conversion.

## Features
- **Lead Capture** - Import and create leads
- **Pipeline Management** - Visual kanban board
- **Lead Scoring** - Priority ranking
- **Activity Tracking** - Interaction history
- **Conversion Tracking** - Success metrics

## Navigation
Access via: Main Menu → Leads

## Key Workflows

### Adding New Leads
1. Go to Leads → Add Lead
2. Fill in lead information:
   - Contact details
   - Company information
   - Source (website, referral, etc.)
   - Initial requirements
3. Assign lead owner
4. Set priority level
5. Save the lead

### Managing Pipeline
1. View kanban board
2. Drag leads between stages:
   - **New**: Initial capture
   - **Contacted**: First outreach made
   - **Qualified**: Viable opportunity
   - **Proposal**: Quote sent
   - **Negotiation**: Terms discussion
   - **Won**: Converted to customer
   - **Lost**: Not converted

### Lead Activities
1. Open lead record
2. Log activities:
   - **Calls**: Phone conversations
   - **Emails**: Communication history
   - **Meetings**: Face-to-face or virtual
   - **Tasks**: Follow-up actions
   - **Notes**: General observations

### Converting Leads
1. Select qualified lead
2. Click "Convert to Customer"
3. Create customer record
4. Transfer relevant data
5. Create initial quote/order

## Lead Stages

### New Leads
- Initial data capture
- Basic qualification
- Assignment to sales rep

### Contacted Leads
- First outreach completed
- Initial response received
- Interest level assessed

### Qualified Leads
- Budget confirmed
- Timeline established
- Decision maker identified

### Proposal Stage
- Requirements gathered
- Quote prepared
- Proposal sent

### Negotiation
- Terms discussed
- Objections handled
- Final agreement

### Conversion
- Deal closed
- Customer created
- Handover to account management

## Lead Scoring
- **High Priority**: Hot leads, immediate follow-up
- **Medium Priority**: Warm leads, regular follow-up
- **Low Priority**: Cold leads, periodic check-ins

## Permissions Required
- View Leads: See lead data
- Edit Leads: Create/modify leads
- Admin Leads: Full access and reporting

## Tips
- Follow up promptly
- Document all interactions
- Use lead scoring effectively
- Regular pipeline review
- Focus on high-potential leads
