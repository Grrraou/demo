# Customer Management Module

## Overview
The Customer module manages customer relationships, contacts, and company information.

## Features
- **Customers** - Individual customer records
- **Customer Companies** - Business accounts
- **Customer Contacts** - Multiple contacts per company
- **Customer History** - Interaction tracking

## Navigation
Access via: Main Menu → Customers

## Key Workflows

### Adding Individual Customers
1. Go to Customers → Individuals
2. Click "Create Customer"
3. Fill in personal information:
   - Name and email
   - Phone number
   - Address
4. Save the customer

### Managing Customer Companies
1. Navigate to Customer Companies
2. Click "Create Company"
3. Enter business details:
   - Company name
   - Business address
   - Industry
   - Contact person
4. Add multiple contacts if needed

### Managing Customer Contacts
1. Select a customer company
2. Click "Add Contact"
3. Enter contact details:
   - Name and title
   - Email and phone
   - Department
   - Role in company

### Viewing Customer History
1. Open customer record
2. View related tabs:
   - Orders history
   - Quotes sent
   - Invoices
   - Communication log

## Customer Data Fields

### Individual Customers
- Personal information (name, email, phone)
- Billing and shipping addresses
- Communication preferences
- Notes and tags

### Customer Companies
- Business information
- Multiple contacts
- Company hierarchy
- Account status

## Permissions Required
- View Customers: See customer data
- Edit Customers: Create/modify customers
- Admin Customers: Full access

## Tips
- Keep contact information current
- Use tags for customer segmentation
- Track all customer interactions
- Link customers to sales orders
- Regular data cleanup
